Owning a Cavoodle

Owning a Cavoodle — or in our case, two — is one of the greatest joys in life. Breefox, our gentle blond boy, brings calmness and warmth into every day, while Xandie, our chocolate-red cuddle lover, fills our home with playful energy and excitement for every walk and adventure. Both are smart, affectionate, and wonderfully low-shedding, making them perfect companions for family life. Breefox and Xandie brighten every moment with their loving personalities and remind us just how special this beautiful breed truly is.

Cavoodle, also known as the Cavapoo, is one of the most beloved designer dog breeds, created by crossing the gentle Cavalier King Charles Spaniel with the intelligent Poodle. This small to medium-sized companion is cherished for its warm, affectionate temperament and friendly nature. In our home, Breefox, now 7 years old, and Xandie, 4 years old, perfectly showcase the charm of this breed with their playful personalities and loving dispositions. Cavoodles thrive in families, with children, or as loyal companions for individuals, adapting easily to different lifestyles and living spaces. With their soft, low-shedding coats and joyful spirits, Cavoodles bring comfort, companionship, and endless love to every home they join.
