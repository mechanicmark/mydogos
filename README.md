Overview

This website is dedicated to celebrating Cavoodles (Cavapoos), a popular designer breed known for their affectionate nature, intelligence, and friendly personalities. The site features general information about the breed, care tips, and a special spotlight on Breefox (7 years old) and Xandie (4 years old), the two Cavoodles who inspired this project.

Features

About the Cavoodle Breed
Detailed explanation of what Cavoodles are, their origins, temperament, and why they make excellent companions.

Meet Breefox & Xandie
A personalised section introducing each dog, including their colours, ages, personalities, and favourite activities.

Care Tips
Guidance on grooming, exercise, training, and general wellbeing for Cavoodles.

Responsive Layout
The website is built to display beautifully on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Photo Sections (Optional Add-ons)
Space for image galleries or individual photos of Breefox and Xandie.

Purpose

This website serves as a friendly, information-rich space for:

Cavoodle lovers

People researching the breed

Visitors who want to learn more about Breefox and Xandie

Anyone seeking simple advice on caring for Cavoodles
